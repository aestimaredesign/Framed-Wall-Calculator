Guidelines & Formulas

This calculator estimates materials and costs for constructing framed walls based on provided parameters.

Wall Area & Material Quantity:
Wall Area = (Length × Height) - (Door Area + Window Area)
Material Quantity = Ceiling(Wall Area / Unit Area of Material) × (1 + Waste Factor%) × Total Plies
Accessories for Framed Walls:
Studs = Ceiling(Length / 0.6) × Height × Total Plies
Tracks = 2 × Length × Total Plies
Screws = Ceiling(Wall Area × 25) × Total Plies
Joint Compound = Wall Area × 0.5 × Total Plies
Sealant = Length × 0.1 × Total Plies
Labor Estimation:
Installation Days = Wall Area / Productivity Rate
Total Days = (Preparation Days + Installation Days + Cleanup Days) / Number of Laborers
Cost Estimation:
Material Cost = Σ(Material Quantity × Cost per Unit)
Accessory Cost = Σ(Accessory Quantity × Cost per Unit)
Labor Cost = Wall Area × Labor Cost per m²
Contingency Cost = (Material Cost + Accessory Cost + Labor Cost) × Contingency%
Overhead Cost = (Base Costs) × Overhead%
Equipment Cost = (Base Costs) × Equipment%
Profit Cost = (Base Costs + Contingency + Overhead + Equipment) × Profit Margin%
Total Cost = Material Cost + Accessory Cost + Labor Cost + Contingency Cost + Overhead Cost + Equipment Cost + Profit Cost
